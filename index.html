Personal Profile

First, let me introduce myself - my name is Andy Fedorchuk, and I hail from Vancouver, BC, Canada. I'm a Designer who loves the production side of game development. For the last 4 months I've been working as a Associate Designer at Relic Entertainment on the Company of Heroes franchise.

Prior to that I served as the class representative for Game Design Class 30 at Vancouver Film School. At VFS I was the level designer and project manager for my final project, Inkling.

I love the game development process, and the combination of art and science that goes into making amazing experiences for players. Testing, iterating, and improving on games during production is incredibly useful to my process, and provides me with a lot of satisfaction.
